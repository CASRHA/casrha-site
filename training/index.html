Common questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the self-paced training.

Who can take this training?

Anyone! Parents, teachers, youth workers, pastors, mentors, or anyone who interacts with children and adolescents. No prior experience required.

Is the training really free?

Yes, 100% free. No hidden fees, no payment required at any stage. This is our gift to you and your community.

Do I need to watch the modules in order?

It's recommended but not required. The modules build on each other, so watching in sequence gives you the best learning experience.

How long does the training take?

Each of the 6 modules is approximately 20-30 minutes. You can complete the entire course in about 2-3 hours, but since it's self-paced, you can spread it over days or weeks.

What is the outreach requirement?

You must engage at least one child, teenager, or a group with what you've learned. This practical deployment can be as simple as a conversation with your own child or a session with a youth group. Submit a brief report with pictures (optional but encouraged).

Can I get a certificate without doing the outreach?

No. The certificate is awarded upon submitting your outreach report. This training is for transformation and deployment, not just information. We want you to apply what you've learned!

How do I download my certificate?

After completing your outreach, submit your report via the certificate portal (link below). Your certificate generates instantly as a PDF or JPEG for download.

What if I have more questions?

Use the live chat widget (bottom-right corner), email us at info@casrhafoundation.org, or join our WhatsApp/Telegram community.
