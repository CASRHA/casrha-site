*🎓 HOW TO GET YOUR CERTIFICATE — 3 SIMPLE STEPS*

Congratulations, Crusader of Wholesome Sex Education! Once you have completed your outreach, your certificate is just minutes away:

*Step 1 — Complete Your Outreach* ✅
Engage a child, teenager, or group in wholesome sex education. Keep records of your session, including pictures and videos if possible.

*Step 2 — Submit Your Outreach Report* 📝
Fill out the short report form here:
https://wse.casrhafoundation.org/certificate-request

📌 *Please note as you fill the form:*
• Type your *Full Name* exactly the way you want it to appear on your certificate.
• Be sure to indicate the *End of Training Day* — this is the date that will be shown on your certificate.
• Made a mistake? No need to panic! Just fill the form again on the same page and generate a fresh certificate.

📸 We also warmly encourage you to upload your outreach pictures to our Telegram group — it's optional, but your pictures will inspire other Crusaders to rise up and reach out too!
https://t.me/casrhafoundation/7018

*Step 3 — Download Your Certificate* 🎓
Once you submit the form, your Certificate of Completion is generated instantly. Download it as PDF or JPEG — it's yours!

God bless you as you rise up and reach out! 🙏
