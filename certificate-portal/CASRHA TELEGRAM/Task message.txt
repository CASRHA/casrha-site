*Greetings to you Crusaders of Wholesome Sex Education, and congratulations on the successful completion of the Training*

As a part of this training, you will need to put into practice what you have learnt.

*You must Rise up and Reach out... There's a Macedonian call. These children and young people are crying, "Come over to our school, our street, our home, our church and help us"*.

You are to prayerfully engage at least a child or teenager, or a group of children and teenagers in sex education over the next few days to weeks. 

Keep records of the session, including pictures and videos, if possible. Share the report of your engagement with us on this group. Afterwards, we will give you access to download your certificate of completion. 

__________
If you plan to visit schools, the following tips will be handy for you. 
__________
1. Prayerfully write down the names of the schools in your neighborhood;

2. Set out a day, preferably first thing in the morning, to visit the schools one after the other to book appointment; tomorrow is another tomorrow and tomorrow has no end, do it right away. 

3. When you get to the school, request to see the Head Teacher or the Principal. You may meet some resistance, don't be discouraged. Push further! 

4. When you are given access, introduce yourself simply starting with your name; eg.

 _I am John BALOGUN, I have come to seek your permission to teach your students Wholesome Sex Education. I have undergone a certificate course on how to teach this. I will like to know when I can come to talk to your students, etcetera._

5. You will either get a Yes or a No. If yes, plan for the day you're given to come. You can reach out to us for guidance and support with necessary materials.

6. If they tell you No, appreciate them and move to the next school. Jesus Christ said, "Move to the next city...". Don't get discouraged. They will send for you later when they hear about your exploits from other schools.

7. Some schools will request an official letter of request, we can give you a template letter you can print and submit to them. Others may request your approval letter from the Education Board, if you have this, submit it to them, otherwise, thank them and move on to the next school.

8. Don't ever get discouraged by any negative response. Keep moving. Signs and wonders will follow you.

We are passionately praying for you as you rise up and reach out to these children and young people.
