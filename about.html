Who we are

Bridging the gap between children and the truth about their bodies.

The Children & Adolescents Sexual and Reproductive Health Awareness (CASRHA) Foundation exists to raise sexually informed children and adolescents — through age-appropriate, culturally relevant and spiritually edifying Wholesome Sex Education.

Since 2019 · Abuja, Nigeria
Our story

A consistent drop makes an ocean

Our children face daily temptation to engage in sexual activity — often before they understand what it is. Research shows that equipping them early with the right knowledge empowers them to take responsibility for their health and escape predators, disease, and premature parenthood.

“Train up a child in the way he should go…” — but many parents and teachers don’t know the way, or miss the right approach and timing. Founded by Dr. John and Mrs. Opeyemi Balogun, CASRHA Foundation exists to provide that missing link: equipping the adults around a child with the tools to teach wholesome sex education with confidence.

What began as a handful of trainings has grown into a movement — over 10,000 parents and teachers trained across 34+ batches, and 200+ school and community outreaches nationwide.

What drives us

Mission, vision & values

The convictions that shape every training we run and every child we reach.

Our mission

To equip parents, teachers and mentors to teach age-appropriate, wholesome sex education — protecting children and adolescents from abuse, exploitation and the consequences of premature sexual activity.

Our vision

A generation of sexually informed children and adolescents — raised in a world where wholesome content saturates the spaces they occupy, and no child falls victim to sexual immorality.

Protection

Every child deserves to recognise, resist and report predators — and to grow up free from abuse and exploitation.

Truth

We teach the truth about the body clearly and age-appropriately — no shame, no silence, no misinformation.

Empowerment

We equip the trusted adults around a child — parents, teachers, mentors — so the teaching outlives any single outreach.

Compassion

We meet children and families where they are — with warmth, cultural sensitivity and grace, never judgement.

Integrity

A registered non-profit that reports every outreach and stewards every gift transparently — free at the point of need.

Faith

Rooted in the conviction to train up a child in the way they should go — spiritually edifying and grounded in enduring values.
