Dr. John S. Balogun is a prominent Nigerian Paediatric Oncologist and Child Health Specialist based in Abuja, Nigeria. He is highly recognized for both his specialized medical work in childhood cancer and his extensive advocacy in youth mentorship, relationships, and sex education.

Dr. Balogun has a comprehensive clinical background focused on pediatric health. He completed his clinical foundation and pediatric medical residency training at the University of Abuja Teaching Hospital (UATH). He advanced through post-graduate medical pathways under the West African College of Physicians.

To specialize in childhood cancers, he undertook advanced oncology clinical fellowships at Korle Bu Teaching Hospital in Accra, Ghana, and the world-renowned Tata Memorial Centre in Mumbai, India.


He maintains active membership in prestigious global medical bodies, including the International Society of Paediatric Oncology (SIOP) and the American Society of Clinical Oncology (ASCO)

Beyond the hospital wards, Dr. Balogun is passionately involved in child and adolescent education; for close to two decades, Dr. Balogun has been mentoring youths since he was 19 years old, a calling that comes very naturally to him. 

He is a seasoned, gifted teacher of God’s word and a sexual purity crusader. with a certification in Pragmatic Approaches for Youth and Adolescent Sexual and Reproductive Health. He uses every opportunity to reach out and help people to know the Lord Jesus Christ, without pursuit of prize or reward. He worships with Deeper Life Bible Church and currently serves as the Youth Leader of the church's Quarters Group of Districts in Gwagwalada, FCT, Nigeria.

Early Life and Education:
Dr. Balogun was born in Ondo State, Nigeria. He grew up as the 5th of 14 children in a polygamous family, being the first son and the 4th of 8 children born to his mother. He started living alone at the age of 11 due to his mother's decision to ensure he obtained a quality education that was unavailable in their village.

His academic dedication bore fruit when he graduated as the best graduating student from Beulah Baptist High School in Ore. He went on to earn his Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery degrees from Nnamdi Azikiwe University in Anambra State, where he was a beneficiary of the MTN Foundation Science and Technology Scholarship throughout his university years.  

Throughout his life, Dr. Balogun has lived in various parts of Nigeria, including Ore in Ondo State; Omu-Aran and Offa in Kwara State; and Awka and Nnewi in Anambra State. He currently resides in Abuja. While he has lived only in Nigeria, his travels have taken him to other African and Asian nations, including visits to Addis Ababa, New Delhi, and Chennai.

Advocacy and Foundations
Dr. Balogun is a Crusader of Sexual Purity, a vehicle he and his wife use to teach age-appropriate, culturally relevant, and scientifically accurate sex education. He is the convener of the monthly Wholesome Sex Education training for children's and youth teachers, parents, and child advocates. 

They both founded the CASRHA Foundation, an organization dedicated to promoting Wholesome Sex Education and raising sexual and reproductive health awareness in schools, children's and youth camps, campuses, and religious organizations. More information about their activities can be found at the official CASRHA Foundation Portal. Additionally, they host a monthly neighborhood meeting tagged Hangout with Dr. & Mrs. BALOGUN, where they host preteens, teens, and young adults for discussions on sex education.

Literary Work and Creative Interests
As a prolific writer, Dr. Balogun prefers writing to speaking, though he notes that when pushed to the podium, he can speak extensively. He writes mostly on Christian living, the possibility of premarital and marital sexual purity, relationships, and marriage. To the glory of God, he has written over 30 books. His publications can be accessed at www.bjseyi.wordpress.com/downloads. 

He is a voracious reader who prefers digital books to hard copies, and he learns both actively and passively from the world around him. He also composes songs and currently considers his children as his team members, with plans to release family albums in the future.

Personal Life and Faith
Dr. Balogun describes his temperament as a Sanguine with a phlegmatic touch, noting that his character has been thoroughly refined by his encounter with the Person of Jesus Christ, whose life he now lives. He has learned to carry the presence of God everywhere he goes, which helps him maintain the freshness of the Spirit at all times. His favorite books of the Bible are the Gospel of John, the three epistles of John, and the Epistle to the Ephesians.

He is married to Opeyemi, a children's and youth teacher, professional counselor, and one of his former chemistry students from senior secondary school. They are blessed with two adorable children.
