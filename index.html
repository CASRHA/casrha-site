Who we are

Bridging the gap between children and the truth about their bodies

Our children face daily temptation to engage in sexual activity — often before they understand what it is. Research shows that equipping them early with the right knowledge empowers them to take responsibility for their health and escape predators, disease, and premature parenthood.

“Train up a child in the way he should go…” — but many parents and teachers don’t know the way, or miss the right approach and timing. CASRHA Foundation exists to provide that missing link.

Growing our reach to the whole world

“And he said unto them, Go ye into all the world, and preach the gospel to every creature.”

Over 10,000 parents and teachers have already taken the free Wholesome Sex Education training course — and we are still counting. But our true reach is harder to estimate; it is like asking how many people Jesus Christ has saved since He gave His life for humanity. Only heaven can tell.

By God’s grace, Dr. John S. Balogun began this outreach at the age of 19. On July 20, 2026 he turns 40 — 21 years in this work, to the glory of God. Every year brings a new milestone: new territories, new campuses, new schools and new churches reached with the message of wholesome sex education.
